Hi, my name is Cameron Montgomery.I am an aspiring software developer currently in school learning full-stack development, networking, and cybersecurity. I am a United States Armed Services veteran, having spent five years on active duty in the United States Marine Corps. Currently, I am serving as a director with the 501(c)(3) non-profit, WarriorNOW, which helps our nation's veterans who suffer from mental health, addiction, and judicial involvement. I am fortunate also to hold a seat on the 18th Judicial Problem-Solving Court Steering Committee, where I share my personal experiences to help shape how our judicial system serves those affected by mental health and substance use.
